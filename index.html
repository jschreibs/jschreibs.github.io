We advise you on your coverage, help you shop plans, and advocate for you.
Our Services Are Free for You
The companies we work with pay us a standard, state-determined commission. It never costs you more to work with a GuideStar agent. We search for a plan that works with your doctors, hospitals, medication and income.
How are services free for clients?
Carriers pay StarPlan a standardized commission to advise you on your plan options. CMS determines the compensation carriers give. We just work to find the plan for you.
Can I get a better plan direct-to-carrier?
Plan prices and coverage are fixed by law. You never pay more or less or receive different benefits for a given plan when you work with our advisors.
How do I choose my plan?
There is no “one size fits all” plan option. We help you take into account the differences between premiums and copays, having your doctor in network, what drugs you take, etc.
